Superpowers without Side Effects

Bring Your Own Key (BYOK)

No more paying for overpriced, bundled AI credits. With Superpowers, you connect your own API keys from your favorite providers, giving you full control over your usage and costs.

Open-Source & Transparent

Trust through transparency. Our code is fully open-source and available on GitHub. You can inspect it, contribute to it, and be confident that there are no hidden surprises.

100% Local & Private

Your data never leaves your machine. Superpowers runs entirely locally in your browser, ensuring your conversations and information remain completely private and secure.

Multi-Provider Support

Don't get locked into a single ecosystem. Superpowers is built to support multiple AI providers, giving you the flexibility to choose the best model for any task.

Get Started in 3 Steps

1

Instant Setup

Download the superpowers-x.y.z.zip file from our latest GitHub release. Unpack the zip file and load it as an unpacked extension in Chrome's developer mode.

2

Connect Your Keys

Navigate to the settings panel and securely add your API keys from any supported provider. Your keys are stored locally and are never sent to our servers.

3

Augment Your Browsing

Press Cmd + Shift + U (or Ctrl + Shift + U on Windows/Linux) to open the sidebar, or right-click and select "Use Superpowers" from the context menu.
